Empress Ghuiggd'keg
rating: -10

Item #: SCP-9251
Containment Class: Safe
Clearance Level: SC
Disruption Class: Ekhi
Risk Class: Critical

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9251 is to be kept locked in a basic 5 meter by 5 meter by 5 meter test chamber and all blood is to be kept out of a 3 meter radius. Lethal force is permitted if enough blood is accumulated to initiate the awakening process. All testing has been permanently postponed after the incident written in Addendum A until a way to safely contian SCP-9251-1 has been discovered.

Description: SCP-9251 is a mummy designated SCP-9251-1 that analysis dates it back to 12th century CE and its stone sarcophagus designated SCP-9251-2. While from an initial glance, SCP-9251-1 looks like an average mummy with dried dessicated body, missing internal organs, and wrapped up for burial, there are various anomalies as tests have revealed the fabric used to wrap SCP-9251-1 is not linen or other known fabrics used for mummification but human skin, DNA tests performed on SCP-9251-1 reveals it's not from the homo sapien part of the homonid genus but homo daeva, the hieroglyphics on SCP-9251-2 has been translated as "Our queen shall rise once she has tasted the blood of 200 slaves.", and abnormalities on SCP-9251-1 listed below.

  • Abnormal bone density.
  • Elongated and hollow canines with grooves perfect for injecting venom.
  • Elongated and pointed nails that analysis reveal were cut that way.
  • X-Ray reveal a brain still in SCP-9251-1 with neuron activity despite missing it vital organs.
  • Any human blood that falls onto SCP-9251-1 is instantly absorbed by it.

The last factor is the most alarming part because as SCP-9251-1 consumes blood, it will start to gain a healthier appearance, regrow its lost organs, and ragain moisture until it has consumed 900 to 1100 liter of human blood, which it will appear as a completely healthy woman and will awaken. In the resuscitated state, SCP-9251-1 is extremely hostile and has displayed strength of 200kg, stamina to operate 50 hours without exhaustion, running speed of 60 mph, durability to withstand multiple MTF teams, pain tolerance to ignore multiple injuries, and potent hemokinetic abilities to form blades and projectiles. SCP-9251-1 is vulnerable to the following.

  • Stakes made of human bone.
  • Objects of Sarkic importance.
  • Decapitation.
  • Total destruction of organs.
  • Incineration.
  • Munitions exceeding 50 caliber.

Once its internal organs have been destroyed, incinerated, beheaded, had its heart pierced with a stake, ir injured severely with a Sarkic relic, SCP-9251-1 will expire and begin to rapidly dhydrate and all organs except for the brian to rapidly decompose into nothing. Once this dehydration and decomposition procedure is complete, SCP-9251-1 will demanifest from where it fell and remanifest back within SCP-9251-2.

Addendum: SCP-9251-A
SCP-9251 was found within ruins in a set of Daevite ruins in South-Central Siberia, surrounded by the skeletons arranged in a way that was similar to know Daevite blood sacrifice rituals.

During initial testing, Level 4 researcher Doctor Gusarova Klavdia Denisovna had liters of blood fed to SCP-9251-1 until it reaches 1002 which caused the awakening resulting in the deaths of Doctor Denisovna, her team, and multiple MTF squads before SCP-9251-1 succumbed to its wounds and reverted back to an intert state. After this incident blood has been forbidden from being allowed within three meters of the test chamber.
