Scp 9124

Item #: SCP-9124

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9124 is to be kept in a reinforced hazardous object locker with acid resistant coating.

Description: SCP-9124-1 is a small, black leather tote bag. SCP-9124-1 contains a multitude of conventionally lethal weaponry collectively classified as SCP-9124-2-x ranging from poison to knives. See object log1. The bag is extradimensional in nature, containing far more weapons than a regular bag of its size should be able to. Due to the unknown and possibly infinite amount of weapons stored in the bag, all instances of SCP-9124-2-x are to be contained in SCP-9124-1.

Anomalous Properties: Upon any instance of SCP-9124-2-x directly causing the death of a subject, their corpse is designated SCP-9124-3. In all medical, legal, and biological definitions, SCP-9124-3 is deceased. However, upon 3-6 hours post death, SCP-9124-3 will undergo full cellular rejuvenation, resulting in full immediate revival. After this point, instances of SCP-9124-3 exhibit no lingering anomalous properties following revival, though persistent psychological trauma is frequently reported.

Addendum SCP-9124-A: Test log of SCP-9124-2-682 with SCP-682

D-Class personnel empties the bottle into SCP-682's acid, then throws the bottle in as well before being grabbed and [Data Expunged] by SCP-682
Doctor. ██████ quickly hit the close container button, resealing SCP-682.
SCP-682 began thrashing violently in the acid, before apparently dying.
180 minutes postmortem, SCP-682 revived and grew a thick non-permeable membrane around their entire body.
Another vial of "Instant death juice" was procured from SCP-294, although it did not work a second time as SCP-682's new membrane prevented the substance from causing significant damage, resulting only in the growth of a small localized tumor, which SCP-682 clawed off.

Addendum SCP-9124-B: Interview with SCP-9124-3: Dr. ██████.

Interviewer: Dr. ██████, what was your experience while dead?
Dr. ██████: I didn't feel any time in between. It was just munch(on SCP-9124-2-17, a cyanide capsule) and then immediately I awoke after 273 minutes.
Interviewer: You didn't feel an afterlife or anything of the sort?
Dr. ██████: No. If I did, I assume the memories would stay in the afterlife.
Interviewer: Doctor, please refrain from frivolous speculation. Did you or did you not see what happens after death?
Dr. ██████: I did not experience anything after my temporary death.

Footnotes
1. SCP-9124-2-1: A primitive style flint spear 1.5 meters in length and 1.3 cm across.
SCP-9124-2-2: A 8oz vial of arsenic. The vial has been used 4 times and is now empty.
SCP-9124-2-3: A small smoky gray ceramic dagger. Measures 57.2 BESS.
SCP-9124-2-4: An identical dagger to SCP-9124-2-3.
SCP-9124-2-5: A vat of hydrochloric acid(hence the acid resistant coating for container).
SCP-9124-2-17: A single cyanide capsule, standard appearance. Manifests in a small pill bottle that replenishes upon closure of SCP-9124-1 with the bottle inside, like all SCP-9124-2-x instances that are consumable.
SCP-9124-2-682: A 2oz bottle of fluid labeled "Instant death juice".
