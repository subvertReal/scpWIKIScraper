SCP-9042

A careless gardener sows an eager crop.

Few remember why we turned our eyes from the sky.

When we accepted being alone in the vast universe as the only truth.

When the allure of the stars became nothing but empty ash and swirling rust.

When we returned to holding the wilds of this world as the only frontier remaining.

Perhaps if our eyes had not been forced lower, they would have been lost to us instead.

Not that it matters now. The hands have slipped. The stars are fracturing.

Our siblings await beyond the skies.

Description: SCP-9042 denotes the entirety of the Sol stellar system, excluding the Sun and Earth, following the effects of the Cascade Event.

Timeline of the Cascade Event

Dr. Repara Interrogation I

Interviewed: Dr. Jasmine Fata Repara, former project lead of Prometheus Labs Space Exploration Division.

Interviewer: Head Researcher Amanda Graham, project lead for the Stellar Defense Arm within the Foundation Space Division.

Foreword: This interview took place directly after the apprehension of Dr. Repara and her associates on-site.

<Begin Log, 06/21/30 12:24 UST>

Graham: Hello, Doctor Repara.

POI-9042 sits forward, her eyes widening.

POI-9042: Really, Amanda? So formal. Tell me, darling, how have you been? You look startled.

She grins. Graham's brow furrows.

Graham: Eigenweapons tend to have that effect.

POI-9042: I thought your ilk would be accustomed to that sort of thing. Didn’t the pitch mention defending the world from chaos and dread? Something like that?

Graham: Yes. Now, can you please explain your intentions? Who are you working for? Why the mixed batch when it comes to your personnel?

POI-9042: Does any of that really matter, Amanda? Your pretty toys destroyed half a decade of work in under a second. Despite that, and all this, it is good to see you again.

Graham: Humor me?

POI-9042: If you agree to do so in turn.

Graham: That will depend entirely on the information you provide.

POI-9042 purses her lips and taps her fingers on the table for several minutes.

POI-9042: I always liked it when you attempted to play hard to get. Go on.

Graham: Your team?

POI-9042: Right, well, I can’t honestly say I am working for any one person. There was a complex network of interested parties involved in my research, which resulted in a diverse group of helpers. It was nice to have different perspectives; I doubt you see that much amongst the Foundation.

Graham: Where are they?

POI-9042: Safe. They did good work, a cold cell being their only reward for all they’d done hadn’t seemed fair.

Graham notes that response and looks back up.

Graham: You got them out?

POI-9042: Of course, as I said, a lot of folk were interested in our work. Maybe they’ll keep at it, but for now, they’re out of your reach.

Graham: Little is beyond our reach.

POI-9042 scoffs.

POI-9042: They’ve hooked you good and proper, haven’t they?

She leans towards Graham, her eyes narrowed. Graham focuses on her writing.

Graham: What were your intentions for the eigenweapon?

POI-9042: Now, now, I thought we agreed on a tit for tat!

Graham: I suppose we did, then. Please, what do you wish to know, Dr. Repara?

POI-9042: Again, so formal. Hmm, how about, do you still remember what you told me in Portland?

Graham: That… I never wanted the night to end. Because I knew it was all going to change after that.

She leans away from POI-9042. Their eyes narrow.

POI-9042: Mmm, I remember some other aspects about that night, like-

Graham: Please, back to the eigenweapon.

POI-9042 wrings her hands.

POI-9042: Well, honestly, I think using that term is a bit extreme regarding my device.

Graham: You aimed a clock-tower-sized bullet at the sun with enough thaumic energy in it to shake the noosphere. What would you call it then?

POI-9042 blinks, tilting her head slightly.

POI-9042: A vaccine.

Graham: For what? Sol itself?

POI-9042 scoffs.

POI-9042: No sickness exists for the sun other than rage; you should know that. No, the sun was to be my assistant in the inoculation process.

Graham: For what?

POI-9042 holds her arms wide.

POI-9042: For all this.

Graham: The Foundation?

POI-9042: No, no, though your organization has not been very helpful in regards to the plights our world is facing. We were hoping to salve at least a few wounds.

Graham: How exactly?

POI-9042: Again, Amanda, I have to ask, does this actually matter to you? If I remember you correctly, this should make you quite happy. A great change halted.

Graham grimaces.

Graham: An act of great chaos, if you had succeeded. The question remains.

POI-9042: If indeed. Now, my dreams lie dead amongst the stars, and hopes lie unnoticed at your feet. I’m sure your superiors can find the answers on their own, as plain as they are.

Graham rolls her eyes.

Graham: How dramatic.

POI-9042 leans back in, reaching for Graham's hand.

POI-9042: You liked it when I was dramatic.

Graham retracts her hand, yet POI-9042 keeps her extended.

Graham: Are you going to answer the question?

POI-9042: Unlikely. You no doubt have my memos, my notes, why lay it all out when you can find it yourself?

Graham: Well. Then for fairness' sake, more than anything, would you want to ask me anything more?

POI-9042 stares at her for a moment, chewing on her lip.

POI-9042: Why not, Researcher Graham? Are you still stuck on the idea that you can build a wall against change? Did the Foundation assist you in finding it?

Graham: In a way.

POI-9042 finally retracts her hand.

POI-9042: Shame. I had hoped you’d learned by now. Well then, I think it’s time for you to leave. I wish to mourn my last great endeavor.

<End Log, 12:42 PM UST>

Update 08/03/30: SCP-9042-A is the designation for a series of techno-organic structures detected on/within three seven nine all planetoids, dwarf planetoids, and a selection of larger asteroids within the Sol system, excluding Earth. Their forms are varied, though each resembles, in a fashion, a terrestrial form of flora. They are composed of a hybridization of nonterrestrial metals resembling the composition of SCP-████ terrestrial foliage, namely, trees. Sampled examples include holly, oak, banyan, ginkgo, and cypress.

SCP-9042-A’s primary function appears to be the recreation or origination of biospheres within the planet to which they are attached. Part of the process appears to be thaumic in origin, while other aspects rely on solar or geothermal power on the more terrestrial worlds, with wind power common amongst the gas giants.

The emergent biospheres created by SCP-9042-A are evolving at an extremely rapid pace.

A secondary ability to manifest mobile avatars that attempt to immediately terminate individuals who cause damage to the SCP-9042-A structures has been recorded. Outside of this defense system, SCP-9042-A has proven resilient, resisting outright or regenerating from all current termination attempts by current Foundation personnel. POI-9042 blinks, her pupils narrowing.

POI-9042’s eyes become like slits, and her breathing falters.

POI-9042: You… know?

POI-9042: Amanda… is something as trite and artificial as normalcy something your organization is genuinely prepared to commit omnicide over?

POI-9042: Most?

Addendum 9042.3 - Project Svalinn

PROJECT SVALINN

Description: Project Svalinn is a three-part initiative created for the sole purpose of defending Earth and the non-anomalous population from the existence of SCP-9042. While the study of these entities will continue regarding their possible containment and termination, this will grant our organization the time to do so.

Or so is the hope.

Initiative I - Dead Skies: This initiative is to be undertaken immediately: a global amnestic and propaganda campaign that erodes public confidence in space travel and the veracity of claims regarding life outside Earth, while also increasing long-term disinterest in the night sky. This procedure aims to limit humanity’s curiosity about the stars and facilitate the dissolution of organizations and jobs tied to space exploration and study through a combination of false flags and public pressure.

The hope is that refocusing humanity’s consciousness away from stellar interests, alongside campaigns regarding climate change, will lead to a heightened focus on repairing the ongoing issues on Earth.

A quarter of the Foundation’s resources will be anchored in perpetuating this obfuscation on top of administering to any SCPs on Earth that will not be worked into the remainder of the Svalinn Project, while also continuing to seek a solution to the SCP-9042 dilemma.

Initiative II - Tip of the Spear: This initiative is to commence immediately. Entrenched positions outside of Earth are to be dissolved as efficiently and promptly as possible. All essential materials are to be stripped, and any manageable SCP instances stored on site are to be evacuated as well. Those deemed too dangerous to transport must be placed under long-term lockdown, with automated personnel responsible for implementing workable protocols.

Half of this personnel are to consolidate with the joint Foundation-GOC fleet currently orbiting Earth. Afterwards, the fleet will employ coordinated strikes against the SCP-9042 instances, targeting key locations and doing everything in their power to slow their progress.

Numerous eigenweapons and SCP instances have been authorized for use during this process. Secondarily, the gathering of required resources from SCP-9042, namely SCP-9042-5, falls under their jurisdiction.

Initiative III - The Last Wall: Construction of this aspect of Project Svalinn is already underway. The ultimate goal is to create an artificial thaumic shield that surrounds the entire Earth.

The exterior will be reinforced with the remainder of the fleet. At the same time, anti-ontological energies and theological protections are inscribed across the exterior and readministered on a tight cycle by thaumic practitioners and exorcists under the Foundation's employ.

The interior will be covered with a projection of the sky prior to the emergence of SCP-9042, drawing from Foundation video logs with continuous AIC alterations working in conjunction with the efforts of the Dead Skies protocols to keep the Veil in place.

The shield’s power source is also under construction and requires the immediate transfer of all Type-Green material within Foundation custody.

Additionally, connected to all protocols within Project Svalinn is the acquisition of every space-based organization, both national and private, by the Foundation, anomalous or otherwise, by any means available. Individuals in the employ of these organizations will be vetted for their skill and trustworthiness, being brought into the Foundation or amnesticized accordingly.

Resistance is to be expected. Delegates to the various Nexus-Points have been sent, as their extra-dimensionality may prove a risk to the stability of Project Svalinn. Foundation occupation may be required, or the termination of their connection locus. Other GOI, such as the CI or Serpent’s Hand, are likely to be resistant to our plans. Terrestrial MTF forces are officially on standby.
——
This is it—our last Hail Mary.

I know some folks argued for a more proactive end to this conflict. Planet crackers, Type Greens, weaponized Keters — I heard it all, and so too, I imagine, did the O5s. But I am thankful they took my protestations to heart. We have no idea what effects such devastating actions could have, or, in truth, if they would have any effect at all, and not just leave us with even more problems to clean up.

Our observations indicate that the instances are in motion, but most have a considerable distance to travel. This means we have time to halt their progress, if even momentarily, or draw them back “home”. We stay on our toes, and it will keep them on their toes, or so is the hope.

I imagine many of you are wondering why you were recalled to Earth. The answer is evident, yet in three parts, the first is that Luna is our biggest problem, close as she is, and we need all hands to keep her at bay.

The second being, in regard to our farthest outposts, Ceres is closing ranks, threading the asteroid field together into a vast net that grows more and more by the day.

We are not entirely cut off; there are ways out, but for now, Earth truly will be the lone home left to us.

And, thirdly and most importantly, the SCP-9042 instances are coming for her. From their own words, they seek their sister. Humanity has done a number on her, and when they arrive, make no mistake, no matter our actions, they seem likely to reach us. We can't let them find her in this state.

Therefore, healing her must be the priority, on top of uncovering anything we can use to halt their advance. The Veil will stand, on altered and shaking foundations, but it will hold. We will make sure of it.

We can not kill them, but we can maim them. We will become like locusts, tearing away and dying in the dark of the void while the rest of humanity lives under the light of a false sun.
