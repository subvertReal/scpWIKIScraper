SCP-9076
rating: +34

Item #: SCP-9076

Object Class: Thaumiel

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9076 is stored within the active research and production wing of Site-23. A capture system has been installed within the reliquary to harvest the enzymes produced by the object for usage in synthesizing SCP-9076-A.

SCP-9076-A is approved for field usage in doses of up to 150 mg per day, for a maximum of up to 5 days consecutively before a required detox period of one week.

Description: SCP-9076 is the preserved amygdala of an adult human brain contained within a small bronze reliquary, allegedly that of Saint Gereon of Cologne.1 While the specific origin of SCP-9076 cannot be confirmed, the object demonstrates Akiva Radiation levels consistent with that of confirmed relics of catholic saints, and carbon dating indicates the object originates from circa 307 A.D.

If at any time SCP-9076 is present within the legal borders of the same nation as any demonic entity or persons who worship such, it will begin to anomalously excrete a unique enzyme at a rate of 2-6 ml per minute. The processed version of this enzyme is referred to as SCP-9076-A.

SCP-9076-A is a parapharmaceutical compound that, when administered intravenously to a human subject, temporarily increases the subject's resistance to harmful effects of demonic or thaumaturgic origin. The potency of SCP-9076's effects increases relative to both the size and regularity of the applied dosage, progressing through several distinct stages. If at any time the subject performs actions they2 deem as amoral while under the effects of SCP-9076-A, the effects immediately cease.

Stage Dosage Effects
1 25-50 mg, single dose Subject displays Akiva Radiation levels with a 22% increase from baseline. Subject becomes immune to bodily possession and other compulsion effects from affected sources.
2 60-85 mg, single dose Subject displays Akiva Radiation levels with a 31% increase from baseline. In addition to previous effects, subject can visibly perceive the presence of demonic entities and any effects originating from them.
3 100-120 mg, single dose Subject displays Akiva Radiation levels with a 60% increase from baseline. In addition to previous effects, physical contact with the subject has a deleterious effect on demonic entities comparable to 0.925 grade sterling silver.

SCP-9076-A has been proven to be non-addictive. However, usage at stage 3 and beyond has been observed to frequently trigger a pleasure response. Research indicates that the response is psychological in nature rather than neurological, due to the absence of the requisite chemicals to elicit the release of dopamine and other pleasure-signaling compounds in SCP-9076-A.

Stage Dosage Effects
4 100-120 mg per day for 3-6 days Subject displays Akiva Radiation levels with a 104% increase from baseline. In addition to previous effects, the subject develops mildly enhanced mental and physical capabilities and can detect spoken falsehoods.
5 140-170 mg per day for 5-8 days Subject displays Akiva Radiation levels with a 172% increase from baseline. In addition to previous effects, the subject develops mild regenerative properties. The regenerative effect is noted to be over four times as potent when restoring nervous tissue.


Addendum 1: On 11/08/2002, six field agents equipped with several pre-prepared doses of SCP-9076-A to aid in the recontainment of SCP-████ were struck by a rockslide in Wādī Bīmah gorge, Oman. The only survivor of the rockslide was Agent Bottin, who was trapped in the gorge with an unknown number of hostile demonic entities. During this time, Bottin utilized the team's supplies of SCP-9076-A in order to survive, applying dosages significantly in excess of the maximum tested at the time.

Agent Bottin's remains were discovered on 12/04/2002, emanating Akiva Radiation at a level consistent with that of SCP-9076. The following notes were found on the body, and are currently under analysis to identify the progression of symptoms caused by excessive SCP-9076-A usage.

When Bottin's headless remains were uncovered, there were no demonic entities in the gorge. The surrounding area was searched, but no traces of the entities were found. Civilians reported seeing a glowing object an estimated 20 cm across moving through the sky five days before the remains were uncovered. One eyewitness described the object as having "stringy wings, like a jellyfish".
