SCP-7112

A tale of love, forgotten.

rating: +292

SCP-7112.

Item #: SCP-7112

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-7112 is stored in a low-priority containment locker.

Description: SCP-7112 is a hand-knit wool sweater, blue and pink in color. When worn by a human subject, it will apply gentle pressure to the body, which has been reported as similar to receiving a hug or being comforted. SCP-7112 was originally discovered and confiscated during a raid on Madeline McElroy's apartment, a known Gamers Against Weed member.

The raid was authorized due to suspicion that McElroy was planning on releasing a memetic agent on Site-55's personnel. Following interrogation, this suspicion was found to be false. However, SCP-7112 was found among her possessions and McElroy admitted to possessing minor knowledge of the anomalous. She was administered Class-B amnestics and all memory of her involvement with the anomalous was removed. Additionally, McElroy was permitted to return to the Gamers Against Weed chatroom after her laptop was outfitted with tracking software.
